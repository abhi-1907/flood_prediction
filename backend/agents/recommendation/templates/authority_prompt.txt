### Part 1: Authority Recommendations (5–10 items)

{{
  "recommendations": [ ... ],
  "resource_plan": [ ... ]
}}

- Use formal, authoritative language suitable for government officials.
- Be specific about quantities, locations, and timelines.
- If risk is CRITICAL, recommend immediate evacuation and NDRF deployment.
